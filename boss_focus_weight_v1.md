# Boss Focus Weight v1

Version: v1
Status: FINAL
Scope: Combat Prototype

---

Purpose

Improve:

«Boss readability»

without breaking:

- low-fatigue combat
- readability-first philosophy
- Semi Turn-Based Combat direction

Boss should feel:

«important and dangerous»

NOT:

«noisy or cinematic»

---

Core Philosophy

Boss should NOT permanently dominate attention.

Boss should:

«naturally emerge as a focus event»

Formal direction:

«Boss Focus Emergence»

Players should naturally feel:

«“Boss is about to do something.”»

NOT:

«“The game is forcing me to watch.”»

---

Rule 1 — Queue Priority

Boss Skill:

«highest queue priority (S Priority)»

Boss focus events must always appear first in Focus Queue.

Priority order:

Boss Skill

«Elite Skill
Player Skill
Important Heal
Background Event»

---

Rule 2 — Queue First Slot Weight

When:

«first slot in queue = Boss»

Allowed:

Micro Scale Up

Recommended:

110% ~ 120%

Purpose:

«naturally attract attention»

Forbidden:

- giant scaling
- bouncing animation
- exaggerated zoom

---

Subtle Brightness Increase

Recommended:

+10% ~ +20%

Direction:

«subtle glow»

Purpose:

«indicate current focus»

Forbidden:

- flashing
- raid-warning feeling
- strong bloom

---

Light Pulse（Optional）

Recommended:

slow pulse

Cycle:

0.8s ~ 1.5s

Purpose:

«subtle attention guidance»

Forbidden:

- fast blinking
- warning-light behavior

---

Rule 3 — Boss Ready Weight

When:

«Boss skill is approaching ready state»

Allowed:

Micro Brightness

Boss body:

+5% ~ +10%

---

Slight Presence Enhancement（Optional）

Very subtle:

- silhouette readability
- contrast increase

Purpose:

«easier boss recognition»

Forbidden:

- glow pillar
- dramatic shader
- heavy VFX

---

Rule 4 — Boss Target Skill

When:

«Boss uses targeted skill»

Keep:

«Target Line»

Target line rules:

- short duration
- subtle
- readable
- low opacity

Purpose:

«players understand danger»

Forbidden:

- laser line
- permanent indicator

---

Focus Timing

Boss focus exists only:

«around dangerous moments»

Flow:

Boss Ready
→ subtle focus emergence
→ skill trigger
→ focus ends
→ returns to background layer

Boss should NOT:

«permanently dominate screen attention»

---

Success Condition

Players naturally understand:

- boss is preparing a skill
- boss is currently dangerous
- who is targeted

without fatigue.

---

Forbidden Direction

Forbidden:

- cinematic pauses
- forced camera
- giant warning effects
- MMORPG raid alert UI
- permanent boss spotlight

Formal direction:

«dangerous but comfortable readability»
