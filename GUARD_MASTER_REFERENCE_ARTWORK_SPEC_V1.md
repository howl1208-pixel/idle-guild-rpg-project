# GUARD_MASTER_REFERENCE_ARTWORK_SPEC_V1

Status: Review Draft

Purpose:

Create the artwork specification for the first approved Guard Master Reference.

This document defines the target appearance before any artwork generation begins.

No artwork is generated by this document.

---

## Scope

Applies only to:

Promotion 1

Guard

No higher promotions.

---

## Character Identity

Young Kingdom Guard

Recently enlisted professional soldier.

Not yet a veteran.

Not yet a knight.

Should feel trustworthy rather than legendary.

---

## Visual Target

Overall impression:

- Clean
- Disciplined
- Professional
- Grounded
- Kingdom military

Avoid fantasy exaggeration.

---

## Equipment

### Weapon

- Straight Sword

### Shield

- Standard Kingdom Kite Shield

### Armor

- Basic steel plate
- Leather straps
- Simple blue cloth

### Helmet

- Kingdom infantry helmet

### Cape

- Short blue cape

---

## Visual Quality

Must clearly read at:

- Illustration size
- Battlefield adaptation
- Future sprite scale

---

## Signature Elements

Must include:

- Kite Shield
- Straight Sword
- Blue Kingdom Cloth
- Heavy Armor Foundation

---

## Evolution Space

Leave sufficient room for future growth.

Do not use:

- Royal decorations
- Legendary craftsmanship
- Heroic cape
- Elite engravings

These belong to later promotions.

---

## Review Target

The approved artwork becomes:

The permanent parent reference for Promotion 2.

No alternative parent references should exist.

---

## Status Rule

Do not promote to Active Rule.
