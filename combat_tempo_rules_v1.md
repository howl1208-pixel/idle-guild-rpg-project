# Combat Tempo v1

Status: FINAL  
Scope: Global Combat System

## Core Philosophy

Combat is a comfortable-to-watch adventure journey.

Formal direction: `Slow Readable Action`.

- Readable.
- Rhythmic.
- Weighty.
- Seamless.
- Never visually chaotic.

## Duration Targets

- Normal battle: approximately 35 to 55 seconds.
- Elite battle: approximately 50 to 75 seconds.
- Boss battle: approximately 90 to 180 seconds.

## Encounter Flow

- Inter-encounter pause: approximately 0.5 to 1 second.
- Normal enemies enter naturally in approximately 0.2 to 0.6 seconds.
- Elite enemies receive a restrained approximately 0.5 to 1 second presence moment.
- Bosses receive a 2 to 4 second arrival moment without cutscenes or black screens.

## Action Rhythm

- Normal attack: approximately 1.5 to 2.5 seconds.
- Active skill: approximately 8 to 15 seconds.
- Boss skill: approximately 12 to 25 seconds.

Normal attacks remain the primary damage source. Active skills are identifiable lower-frequency moments.

## Battlefield And Presentation

- Maximum party presence: 4 characters plus 1 summon.
- Maximum enemy presence: 6 enemies.
- Effects serve unit and skill readability.
- Ordinary damage numbers remain low-frequency.
- Buff and Debuff displays collapse when needed.
- Camera remains fixed outside restrained major-skill or Boss pressure feedback.

## Settings

Presentation modes are offered without payment:

- Full.
- Simplified (recommended).
- Minimal.

## Final Rule

Combat must remain readable, comfortable, seamless, and full of adventure feeling.
