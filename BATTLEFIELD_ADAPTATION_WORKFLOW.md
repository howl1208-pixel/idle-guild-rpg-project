## Battlefield Adaptation Workflow

### Step 1

Use only an Official Master Reference.

Rejected candidates are never allowed.

### Step 2

Convert the approved character into the Battlefield Bible viewing rules.

Apply:

- 35° Floor Direction
- Rear-dominant View
- Back Surface Priority
- Battlefield Scale
- Safe Area

### Step 3

Perform Battlefield Readability Review.

Verify:

- Profession recognition
- Weapon recognition
- Shield recognition
- Silhouette clarity
- Color readability

### Step 4

Compare against previous promotion.

Questions:

Can players recognize:

"This is the same character."

Can players recognize:

"This character has evolved."

Both answers must be YES.

### Step 5

Perform Scale Verification.

Review at actual battlefield display size.

Do not evaluate using high-resolution artwork only.

### Step 6

Only after Battlefield Review passes:

The character may proceed to Sprite Production.

## Battlefield Review Categories

Review:

1. Profession Identity
2. Character Continuity
3. Evolution Visibility
4. Battlefield Readability
5. Rear-dominant Recognition
6. Scale Recognition
7. Equipment Recognition
8. Overall Impression
